Map Sheets (gvSIG EIEL printing plugin)

The Deputación de Pontevedra in association with Dirección Xeral de Sostibilidade e Paisaxe de la Consellería de Medio Ambiente, Transporte e Infraestruturas de la Xunta de Galicia have funded this project, a gvSIG Desktop extension that provides a new functionality consisting in the automatic creation of series of maps covering a certain area of interest. More details in the gvSIG Map Sheets website
